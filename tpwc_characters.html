The Paths We Choose has a cast of characters originating from locales all over the world. Instead of being confined to one place, the story spans multiple countries and locations across the planet.

People within the universe of The Paths We Choose can be divided into four major groups, as follows:

  • centaurs: a person with a four-legged lower half resembling a land mammal.

  • pescapeds: a person with a four-legged lower half resembling a fish or other sea creature.

  • satyrs: a person with a two-legged lower half resembling a land mammal.

  • merfolk: a person with a fishlike tail as a lower half rather than legs.

In terms of reproduction, centaurs and pescapeds can produce offspring. Satyrs and merfolk may also produce offspring, but it is less common.

Below is a list of all the characters in The Paths We Choose that have concepts and designs.

  • Dustin Clayton: A privateer captain working for Atlantic Atlas. He is loud, brash, somewhat annoying, and quick to jump into a fight, yet loyal to a fault.

  • Tanaka Hikari: A freelance painter. He is Dustin's partner, and frequently travels with the crew on their journeys. Hanae's brother.

  • Ainsley "Meridian" Ross: A privateer first mate working for Atlantic Atlas. She's confident and proud, and can sometimes come across as self-centered. Truth is, she wears her heart on her sleeve, much to her own chagrin.

  • Martin Hokta: A navigator working for Atlantic Atlas. He unfortunately has very little of what most people would call 'common sense', yet he somehow always knows where he is and never gets lost.

  • Oonagh Lilya: A manager and business director at Atlantic Atlas. She is commanding and imposing, so much so that sometimes employees refer to her as 'Boss' instead of her name. Has a tendency to stay quiet about things that, logically, she really should bring up to the upper echelons.

  • Delphina Cargae: Atlantean guardian and high-profile council member. Delphina is soft-spoken, but anyone wtih half a brain knows not to trifle with her. Alirian's sister.

  • Alirian Cargae: Atlantean guardian and council member. He tends to keep to himself, and prefers hunting for fish to boring council meetings. Delphina's brother.

  • Estevao "Neptune" Alden: Atlantean high-profile council member. Their official title is Evening Minister; they attend to and manage citywide events that occur after sunset. Often neglects their own health in order to attend to their duties.

  • Mycun Clarke: A mer clairvoyant and diver. Very spiritual and in tune with herself. She has a strong connection with her family.

  • Jingyi: A well-learned literati. Jingyi owns a very successful bookstore in Honghu, China. To them, living life 'in the fast lane' is the true waste of time.

  • Amka: A kind and caring mother figure, Amka is highly respected in her village, Kangirsuk. She is passionate about the preservation of Inuit culture and passes down folk traditions and stories to the local children.

  • Tanaka Hanae: A pescaped dancer, entertainer, and geisha working at a high-profile theater in Tokyo. Hikari's sister.
