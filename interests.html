time waits for no one

in this section you'll find information relating to the hobbies and fandoms i enjoy, as well as the things i create (o^▽^o) if nothing else, i am a very passionate person, and my interests are what make my life worth living. (*°▽°*) my passions largely define who i am as a person, so if you understand what i like, you'll better understand me!

i believe that it's important to do your best and enjoy life to the fullest. we all have off days, but as a whole, life is about what you can do to find joy and purpose, not about your day job. unless, of course, your day job makes you feel joyous and purposeful! in short, life is what you make it. you can always do little things to improve your life, even if it seems like everything is going to shit. that's why hobbies and interests are so important- they can be a guiding beacon in your life. they certainly have been in mine.

i would really prefer not to discuss discourse related to my interests. it's stressful for me, and i just want to enjoy the things i want without engaging in fandom arguments. i ask that you please respect this when talking to me!
